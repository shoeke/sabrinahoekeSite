Sabrina Hoeke

I began with a Bachelors in Studio Art but I have always been intrigued with the graphics potential of computers. I had worked in graphic design and then self-studied digital illustration and surface pattern design. As I became interested in website design, I decided to take a class in html /css and I got hooked. To me it was a discovery of a very creative group of people meshing artistic and technical skills and it opened up a whole new world. It fits very well with my love of lifelong learning because there is always more to learn in this field and it's constantly evolving!


Born and raised in Northern California, I spend a lot of time at home enjoying the garden and good weather. I have had the privilege of raising 3 strong-minded and creative kids, with my busy, hard-working husband. We still have a menagerie of rescue-pets (two cats and two large dogs) to keep things interesting in our off hours.

I still enjoy working on and improving my digital illustration skills. Over time I have worked with traditional methods alongside digital ones to try and blend the aesthetics of both. As the technology advances and becomes more creative in itself, the "hand-touch" or bit of wonkiness and non-perfection I like in my work is becoming achievable using just digital approaches. The more I can incorporate illustrations with web and coding, the happier I become!
