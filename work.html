Bugboard Collection

This was an interactive art project. The illustration of the insects in the collection box was completed in Adobe Illustrator. In this way each “box”, or switch can be manipulated through css and jquery. It is imperative that the collection box illustration, and the shaded box illustration line up perfectly.
